About Us

 

Embark on a musical journey with Ranjana Chatterjee, a distinguished music professional whose melodious odyssey encompasses a rich tapestry of experiences as a Singer, Artist at All India Radio, and a revered Music Coach.

With a voice steeped in two decades of teaching music, performing as an artist, and coaching aspiring talents, Ranjana embodies the essence of musical excellence. The journey began at the University of Calcutta, where a Master of Music (Gold Medalist) laid the foundation for an illustrious career.

As an artist on the renowned All India Radio platform, Ranjana has not only showcased vocal prowess but has also displayed a remarkable versatility, navigating the diverse landscapes of Hindustani Classical and Bollywood genres. With a commitment to excellence, she has honed skills in vocal techniques and harmonium playing, offering a holistic approach to musical education.

With a robust experience of nearly 3 years in personalized, online music teaching as a Music Coach at Kiya Learning, Ranjana has sculpted multiple musical journeys. From training students at home to strategically catering plans for global learners in Australia, Dubai, UK, USA, and Singapore, she has become a catalyst for musical excellence.
